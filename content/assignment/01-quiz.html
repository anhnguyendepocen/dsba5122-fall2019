Question 4

Which code produces the average mpg and count for Volkswagen manufactured cars by the car’s cylinder (cyl):

## # A tibble: 3 x 3
##     cyl avgHwy count
##   <int>  <dbl> <int>
## 1     4   30.9    17
## 2     5   28.8     4
## 3     6   24.8     6
mpg %>%
  filter(manufacturer == "volkswagen") %>%
  group_by(cyl) %>%
  summarise(avgHwy=mean(hwy), count=n())

  where(manufacturer == "volkswagen") %>%
  select(cyl) %>%
  summarise(avgHwy=mean(hwy), count=n())

  summarise(avgHwy=mean(hwy), count=n()) %>%
