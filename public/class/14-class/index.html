Announcements

Final Projects. Due next class 12/9.

Your final project will have three deliverables due on Canvas:

  • Your app (e.g., as a zip file, github, RStudio.Cloud link)

  • Your presentation slides (e.g., pptx or zip file with xaringan)If you submit xaringan, I highly recommend submitting a .pdf that you can get by viewing your xaringan in full screen, then right click > “print” and then save as pdf.

  • Your final report (e.g., word doc, pdf, html RMarkdown output)

All are due by 6:00pm next Monday (12/9). Please make sure to submit well in advance.

For any of the three projects, you may also provide URL’s but files are the safest bets. You will not be permitted to make any updates after that point.
